A propos

The Gare des Glaciers is a multi-purpose space for rent in Chamonix Mont-Blanc, France. Ideal for group activities, it has a big loft area and can accomodate as many as 25 people to sleep.

Built for the olympic games in 1924, the Gare des Glaciers was the first cable-car station at the Aiguille du Midi. Located in the Pélerins area, you are only 10 minutes away from the city centre and have a unique view on Mont Blanc, the glacier and the sourrounding summits.
